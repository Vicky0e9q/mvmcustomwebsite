How to Transform Your Home with Trendy Tile Flooring

John Smith , January 1st, 2017

If you're looking to transform your home, one of the most effective ways to do so is by updating your flooring. And when it comes to flooring trends, trendy tile flooring is definitely at the top of the list. With so many different styles and designs to choose from, it's easy to find a look that suits your personal style and fits within your budget. In this blog post, we'll explore some of the latest trends in trendy tile flooring and provide tips on how to incorporate them into your home.

Why Choose Trendy Tile Flooring?


There are many reasons why tile flooring is so popular among homeowners. One of the biggest benefits is that it's incredibly durable and long-lasting. Unlike other flooring materials, such as carpet or hardwood, tile is resistant to scratches, dents, and stains, making it a great option for high-traffic areas. Additionally, tile flooring is very easy to clean and maintain, requiring only occasional sweeping and mopping to keep it looking its best.

Another reason why tile flooring is so popular is that it's incredibly versatile. With so many different styles, colors, and patterns available, it's easy to find a look that complements your home's existing décor. From sleek and modern to rustic and charming, there's a tile flooring option for every design aesthetic.

Finally, tile flooring is a great choice for homeowners who are looking to improve their home's value. According to a recent report by the National Association of Realtors, installing new flooring can have a significant impact on a home's resale value. In fact, the report found that homeowners can expect to recoup between 80-100% of the cost of installing new tile flooring when they sell their home.

Trendy Tile Flooring Styles


Now that we've explored some of the benefits of tile flooring, let's take a look at some of the latest trends in trendy tile flooring.

Wood-Look Tile Flooring


One of the hottest trends in tile flooring right now is wood-look tile. This flooring style offers all the beauty of hardwood flooring with the durability and ease of maintenance that comes with tile. And with so many different wood-grain patterns and colors available, it's easy to find a wood-look tile that fits your home's unique style.

Geometric Tile Flooring


If you're looking to make a bold statement with your flooring, consider geometric tile. This trend is all about using tiles in non-traditional shapes, such as hexagons or triangles, to create eye-catching patterns and designs. And with so many different colors and textures available, you can create a truly unique look that complements your home's décor.

Large-Format Tile Flooring


Another trend that's gaining popularity is large-format tile. These tiles are much larger than traditional tiles, measuring anywhere from 12 inches to 48 inches in size. This can create a seamless, modern look that's perfect for open-concept living spaces. And because there are fewer grout lines to clean, large-format tile is also easier to maintain than traditional tile.

Subway Tile Flooring


While subway tile has been around for decades, it's experiencing a resurgence in popularity right now. This classic style is characterized by its rectangular shape and simple, clean lines. And while white is the most traditional color for subway tile, it's also available in a variety of other colors, allowing you to create a look that's uniquely your own.
